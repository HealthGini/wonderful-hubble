📜 Terms of Service & Standards

Effective Date: June 2026

☀️ Our Core Community Mission

gooddeeds.space is built to promote goodness, combat loneliness, and help everyone become the best possible version of themselves—one good deed at a time.

1. Acceptance of Agreement

By creating a profile, browsing stories, or participating in space chat boards, you agree to abide by these Terms of Service. If you do not agree with our uplifting mission or safety policies, please refrain from using the platform.

2. Community Code of Conduct & Dignity

We maintain zero tolerance for discrimination, harassment, hate speech, spam, exploitation, or fraudulent solicitation. Whether seeking support in mental health listening circles, offering educational tutoring, or organizing local mutual aid, all interactions must reflect mutual respect, empathy, and dignity.

3. User Content & Permanent Recognition

You retain ownership of experiences and resources you share. However, to preserve the integrity of community recognition, published celebration Kudos and Posts pass through confirmation steps and become permanent public records of goodness.

4. Safety & Senior Accessibility

Our pilot community prioritizes accessible, safe spaces for individuals of all ages, including seniors. Users are prohibited from sharing misleading medical advice or exploiting vulnerable members. Account administrators reserve the right to remove non-compliant accounts.

5. Disclaimer & Support Portal

gooddeeds.space provides peer collaboration "as is" and does not substitute for professional licensed medical, psychiatric, or emergency services. For platform assistance, please use our built-in Contact portal.
